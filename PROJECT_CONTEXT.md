# Frangit Systems Project Context

## Project Overview

Frangit Systems is an IT & Digital Services venture focused on delivering professional technical support, diagnostics, maintenance, optimization, and preventive technology consulting for individuals and small to medium-sized businesses.

The initial goal of this repository is to build a professional landing page or catalog website that presents the brand clearly, supports future growth, and can later expand into automation-based services such as an Instagram bot.

## Founder Context

- Background: technologist in systems
- Experience: around 4 years in IT support, systems analysis, maintenance, diagnostics, and user assistance
- Tools and platforms used: Azure Active Directory, Microsoft 365, SharePoint, Power Apps
- Additional strengths: hardware installation, domain-joined equipment setup, basic process automation, and corporate environment management
- Current stage: formalizing the business while unemployed, with existing traction from 2 companies and individual clients
- Main objective: create a stronger professional image to attract more business clients

## Services

- Technical diagnostics for computers and related equipment
- Preventive and corrective maintenance
- Performance optimization
- Technology support for small and medium-sized businesses
- Preventive and consultative approach, not only repair work

## Work Model

1. Perform an initial technical review
2. Deliver a detailed report for each device
3. Present a commercial proposal including solutions, labor, and possible replacement parts or upgrades

## Product Goals

- Build a professional digital presence for Frangit Systems
- Start with a landing page or service catalog website
- Keep the stack modern, low-cost, and easy to maintain
- Prepare the brand for future automation services
- Support digital lead capture through social channels
- Create a path toward semi-automated client service and qualification
- Enable free or low-cost deployment options

## Technical Decisions

- CMS avoided for now: WordPress was intentionally rejected
- Frontend stack: React + Vite
- Philosophy: open source and low cost
- Hosting target: likely Cloudflare Pages or GitHub Pages
- Maintenance goal: simple and sustainable for a solo founder
- Local environment: Windows with Visual Studio Code and Node.js installed
- Windows note: use `npm.cmd` when PowerShell execution policy interferes with `npm`
- Important constraint: do not develop Node projects inside Google Drive folders on Windows due to archive and sync errors
- Current workspace: local folder on `C:\`

## Expanded Business Vision

The broader objective is to evolve Daniel Cardona from independent technician to a structured and professional technology services provider with a stronger corporate presence, better client capture, and scalable digital processes.

This project is not limited to a website. It is the digital foundation of the business and should support:

- Brand credibility
- Lead generation
- Clear communication of services and work model
- Future automation initiatives

## Website Scope

The website is planned as a landing page or service catalog that should include:

- Company presentation for Frangit Systems
- Service catalog for diagnostics, maintenance, and optimization
- Clear explanation of the work model
- Contact form
- Direct contact options such as WhatsApp and email
- Modern, minimal, and corporate design

## Social Media and Automation Scope

An important future phase of the project is the creation of an Instagram bot or automated message flow that can:

- Respond automatically to incoming messages
- Provide basic information about services
- Explain review or diagnostic pricing
- Guide prospects toward a quote request
- Collect basic lead information
- Redirect qualified prospects to direct contact or scheduling

Example target flow:

1. A client writes through Instagram
2. The bot sends a greeting and presents key services
3. The bot explains basic pricing or review information
4. The bot requests initial client details
5. The bot redirects the conversation to quotation or scheduling
